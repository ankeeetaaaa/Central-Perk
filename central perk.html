CENTRAL PERK NYC

ESTD. SEPTEMBER 20, 1994

Located on “The Street That Never Sleeps” in the city of New York, Central Perk is a New York City institution. Adding some rock and roll to this famous upscale thoroughfare, Central Perk's menu features our local favorite, the Ultimate Cold Coffee, as well as a variety of beverages, snacks and signature cocktails. The connected Perk Shop offers FRIENDS-inspired apparel and collectible merchandise exclusive to Central Perk NYC.

A TRULY UNIQUE DINING EXPERIENCE

The Cafe is located inside a 103-year-old heritage building called “Monica's Apartment.” Great care has been taken to renovate the house to preserve its unique historic elements while adding Central Perk’s unique touch. This truly special dining venue is a multilevel, 5540 square-foot café that includes seating for 50 guests, the iconic couch, and a live entertainment space. The unique, local music scene now has a new arena for performance that boasts a state-of-the-art audio-visual system and a food hub created for the people of NYC.

LOCAL FLAVOR YOU CAN'T MISS

Discover your favorites on the Central Perk menu, which features classic signature dishes like the Jumbo Combo, Classic Cold Coffee, the Legendary Burger, and a host of premium beverages and cocktails. Acknowledging local tastes, the NYC menu has for the first time in USA introduced a range of flatbreads and many local vegetarian favorites such as Vegetarian Hot Dog, Quesadilla Burger, and more. New York is a city where food is almost a religion, and food lovers will be thrilled by the menu that has been specially crafted with local tastes in mind. The sweet and chilled coffee, and the hot and tangy dishes are sure to delight palates across the city.
